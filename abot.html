Our Story: From Challenge to Empowerment

Thriving Moms’ Space was born from a personal journey of transformation and empowerment. Our Founder, Lucy Kariuki, faced a common dilemma shared by many young moms. Fresh out of university and a new mom to a beautiful girl, Lucy found herself at a crossroads. Her career aspirations seemed at odds with her desire to be present for her child’s early years.
