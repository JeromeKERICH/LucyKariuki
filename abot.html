About us

Our Story: From Challenge to Empowerment

Thriving Moms’ Space was born from a personal journey of transformation and empowerment. Our Founder, Lucy Kariuki, faced a common dilemma shared by many young moms. Fresh out of university and a new mom to a beautiful girl, Lucy found herself at a crossroads. Her career aspirations seemed at odds with her desire to be present for her child’s early years. Determined to find a solution, Lucy explored opportunities that would allow her to earn income without sacrificing time with her daughter. Her breakthrough came when a friend introduced her to the world of online writing. This discovery changed everything. Lucy not only found a way to earn money from home but also discovered a passion for digital content creation. As she honed her skills and built her client base, Lucy experienced firsthand the joy of thriving as a mother and professional. Inspired by her journey, Lucy founded Thriving Moms’ Space with a clear vision: to help other young mothers discover their potential and build flexible careers that complement their family life.
